Frequently Asked Questions

What type of power outlets are used on Taniti?

Power outlets on Taniti use 120 volts, the same standard used in the United States.

What language is spoken on the island?

Most residents speak both Tanitian and English, especially in tourist areas.

Are businesses open during national holidays?

Taniti observes many national holidays, during which tourist attractions and restaurants may be closed. Visitors should plan activities in advance during holiday periods.
