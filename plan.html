Travel Information

Transportation

Getting to Taniti

By Air

Visitors arrive at Taniti City International Airport.

By Cruise Ship

Ships dock at Yellow Leaf Bay near the city center.

Getting Around

  • Public buses run every 30 minutes.
  • Taxis are available in Taniti City 24/7.
  • Rentals: cars, mopeds, and bicycles.

Local Tips

Safety Information

  • Taniti is generally very safe; crime rates are exceptionally low.
  • A modern hospital is located in Taniti City.
  • The island has several smaller clinics for non-emergencies.

Currency & Money

  • The official currency is the U.S. dollar (USD).
  • U.S. currency is widely accepted at major resorts and restaurants.
  • Major credit cards are accepted by most businesses.

Local Laws & Regulations

  • The legal drinking age in Taniti is 18.
  • Alcohol can only be served until midnight in residential zones.
  • Respect all local environmental protections for the rainforest and coral reefs.
